Studios with First Week Free:

Core Power Yoga offers newbies one whole week to try out different classes at their studios. Just sign up online and present your voucher upon arrival to the studio. Don't be late because you can't make a reservation for your first session! These classes are intense and you will sweat so much more than you thought possible

YogaWorks also offers first time clients the opportunity to try out one week of free classes. They mix in weights, cardio, and stretching to amp up the regular yoga class

Studios with First Class Free:

Switch Payground Feel the burn with 20-40 stations that hit every muscle group, packed into a High Intensity Interval Training experience like no other. Every day is different at switch, so it is never the wrong time to try.

Flywheel Sports Flywheel is a forever favorite. Its techy bike makes it tough to be lazy. Personally, I love booking a bike when I have been in a bit of a workout slump because I know I can't cheat this bike. Guaranteed to leave super sweaty everytime.

Epic Hybird Training Epic is a mix of body weight exercises, cardio and strength training with mobility drills to recover and restore your body. It is intense and you will leave feeling so much stronger. This is also a good place to get ideas to add into your own exercise routine at the gym because all of the equipment used is typically found in a gym.

Studios with First Time Discounts and Packages

((305 Fitness)) Don't miss out on their starter 2x1 promo. $34 gets you two chances to #MakeSweatSexy. This class is fun and vibrant. We guarantee you will leave the studio feeelin' yoself.

SLT SLT is like pilates, but on steroids. If you don't want to get super sweaty but still get in an amazing workout this is the class for you. They have a $20 first Class Special

Soulcycle This is everyone's favorite cult workout. We are always checking out their page for special promos, but for now they have a 3x$75 pack for beginners. Although it is still pricy it beats paying the full $36! Make sure you read up on the teacher before. The instructor and music will make or break your ride and your vibe. BTW, don't forget you have to rent shoes for $2 as well.

PROJECT by Equinox Even equinox has found a way to sneak onto the boutique fitness scene! Lucky us though, because they have a BOGO offer we just can't resist.

Rumble Boxing with a side of aceleb sighting. This studio is frequented by Justin Beiber and Hailey Baldwin, and they have a BOGO promo going on. $36 gets you 2x intense boxing sessions.
